Get to Know ME

Me as a QA, a furmom, and a full time mom

Hi there! I’m Aileen, or you can call me "Yen", a dedicated QA professional with a passion for ensuring seamless user experiences in the tech world. But when I’m not immersed in testing software, I’m embracing my favorite roles as a loving furmom to my four adorable cats and a mom who enjoys cooking delicious meals for my kid. This blog is a blend of my professional and personal passions—sharing stories from the tech world, my adventures in the kitchen, and the joy of caring for my furry family. Whether it’s tips on quality assurance, tales of my cats' quirky antics, or recipes my kid can’t get enough of, you’ll find it all here. Thank you for visiting—I can’t wait to share more of my journey with you!

My Space

My four adorable cats, my constant companions, play a huge role in helping me stay calm and focused. Their playful antics, soothing purrs, and unconditional love create a peaceful environment that melts away stress after a long day of testing and debugging. Caring for them teaches me patience and attention to detail—qualities that directly enhance my performance as a QA. Thanks to their comforting presence, I can recharge, maintain a positive mindset, and bring my best self to every project.

  • - I don’t need a Netflix subscription—watching my cats’ daily drama is entertainment enough!
  • - My cats think I work from home just to provide unlimited belly rubs and snacks on demand.
  • - Every cat in my house is an alarm clock… except they don’t have snooze buttons and love 3 a.m. wake-up calls!
  • - Having four cats is like living with tiny, furry managers who silently judge your every move.
  • - I’ve learned that privacy doesn’t exist with cats—my bathroom breaks are now group meetings.
  • - With four cats, every chair is a throne, and I’m just a lowly servant looking for a place to sit.
