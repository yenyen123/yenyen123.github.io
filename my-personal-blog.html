Get to know me

Me as a QA, a furmom, and a full time mom

Hi there! I’m Aileen, or you can call me "Yen", a dedicated QA professional with a passion for ensuring seamless user experiences in the tech world. But when I’m not immersed in testing software, I’m embracing my favorite roles as a loving furmom to my four adorable cats and a mom who enjoys cooking delicious meals for my kid. This blog is a blend of my professional and personal passions—sharing stories from the tech world, my adventures in the kitchen, and the joy of caring for my furry family. Whether it’s tips on quality assurance, tales of my cats' quirky antics, or recipes my kid can’t get enough of, you’ll find it all here. Thank you for visiting—I can’t wait to share more of my journey with you!
